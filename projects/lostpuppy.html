A lost puppy in Paris

  • Tech Stack: Adobe 360
  • View the project here : Project Link

A Lost Puppy in Paris is an immersive 360 project (created by me and my groupmate) that takes the viewer on a journey through the streets of Paris in search of a lost puppy. This project is a perfect blend of technology and storytelling, designed to evoke a range of emotions in the viewer.

It is an adorable and heartwarming story of a puppy named Baxter who loses his best friend Oliver in the streets of Paris. To find Oliver, Baxter will have to rely on his sense of smell and follow his nose through the winding streets of the city.

We adapted this charming story into an immersive 360 video experience, using Adobe to create a fully immersive and interactive world for viewers to explore. With stunning visuals and engaging storytelling, viewers can experience the journey alongside Baxter as he navigates the sights, sounds, and smells of Paris.

The immersive video experience we created using Adobe is a testament to the power of technology to bring stories to life in new and exciting ways. With this project, we hope to inspire viewers and show them the potential of immersive storytelling to create a truly memorable and emotional experience.
